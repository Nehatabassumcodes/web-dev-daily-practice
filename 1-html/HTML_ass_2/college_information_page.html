Sree Dattha Institute of engineering & Science

Sree Dattha Institute of Engineering and Science (SDES) is a premier autonomous college in Telangana, established in 2001 and accredited with a NAAC "A+" grade. Affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad, it offers B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA programs, with engineering admissions based on TG EAMCET ranks. The institution is known for its focus on modern computer science tracks, industry placements, and technical symposiums like Infinity.

Course Duration Eligibility
B.Tech 4 Years TG EAMCET
M.Tech 2 Years GATE
MBA 2 Years TG ICET
